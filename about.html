ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Prottolk : Empowering Industrial Efficiency and Safety through Turnkey Automation Solutions.

Prottolk is a total turnkey solution supplier aimed at improving the efficiency and safety of industrial plants through plant automation. The company is able to supply, install, and commission projects.

The company offers after sales support for new and existing automation installations, through preventative maintenance, specialized customer training and technical support for Variable Speed Drives (VSD’s), Programmable Logic Circuits (PLC’s), Solar Inverters and Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) Switchgears.

Using valuable experience gained in Service engineering, Sales, and Product Management, Prottolk can provide technical and commercial solution-based assistance in the Mining, Power Generation, Solar Energy, FMCG, and Water Industries.

Our Mission

At Prottolk, our mission is to excel in both domestic and international markets by continuously expanding our capabilities throughout the value chain. From concept to commissioning, we strive to deliver exceptional results in contracts we undertake.

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility while ensuring cost-effectiveness and timely delivery.

By building a team of professionals with diverse expertise in project and construction management, design, engineering, finance, and economics, we are poised to seize opportunities in booming local and international markets.

Our Values

Commitment to Quality: Striving for excellence by upholding stringent standards in all aspects of our work.

Safety and Environment: Prioritizing the well-being of our stakeholders and implementing sustainable practices for a greener future.

Cost Economics: Optimizing project costs while maintaining the highest level of quality, ensuring maximum value for our clients.

Timely Delivery: Honoring our commitments by completing projects on schedule, enabling our clients to achieve their goals promptly.

Collaboration and Alliances: Actively seeking and nurturing partnerships to leverage collective expertise and expand our capabilities for mutual growth.
